About Me

Hello! I'm **Preethi Kannan**, a Carnatic music educator, performer, and keyboardist with a lifelong passion for Indian classical music. I began learning Carnatic Keyboard and Vocal at the age of five under the guidance of **Smt. Ranganayaki Gopalan** and **Smt. Shylu Ravindran**, and have been performing on stage since I was ten. Over the years, I have had the privilege of performing alongside renowned maestros and teaching over 50 students in Carnatic Keyboard, Vocal, and Veena through both online and offline classes. My goal is to inspire a love for music while helping every student build a strong foundation, confidence, and performance skills through personalized guidance and practical stage experience.

I teach:

  • 🎹 Carnatic Keyboard
  • 🎼 Western Keyboard
  • 🎤 Carnatic Vocal
  • 🎻 Veena

I also speak Tamil, Telugu, and English, which helps me connect comfortably with students from different backgrounds.

My goal is to make learning music enjoyable while building strong fundamentals and confidence in every student.
